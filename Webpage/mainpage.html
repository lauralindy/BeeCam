Services

Database

The subject of bees is usually not a machine learning one. During our research for this project, we found that there does not exist a public database to store images of beehives for the purposes of research. So we decided to make one. By offering to process images of beehives, we hope to collect a large enough database of images to further technology in the study of bees.

Machine Learning

A neural networks algorithm to recognize cells in the honeycomb and classify them as honey, larva, capped worker, etc.

Step 1. Recognize individual cells as circles and crop a large photo of a honeycomb into individual circular images of cells.

Step 2. Use machine learning to distinguish between the different types of cells.

Android

To bring this to commercial beehive farmers, we wanted to design an android app that made it easier to take and upload photos to our system.
